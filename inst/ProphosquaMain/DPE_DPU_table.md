|                           | **Differential PTM-feature Expression (DPE)**                                                                                           | **Differential PTM-feature Usage (DPU)**                                                                                                 |
|---------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **What it tests**         | Tests raw PTM-feature change between conditions, without any correction for its parent protein.   | Tests **protein normalized** changes of PTM-features.                                                  |
| **When to use**           | To flag PTM-features whose signal changes, even if the protein itself is also up- or down-regulated. | To determine whether the **fraction** of protein that is modified shifts.                              |
| **How it works**          | - Log₂-transform PTM-feature intensities and apply normalization (`prolfqua::LFQTransformer`)  <br>- Fit your usual differential-expression model (`prolfqua::build_model`) | - Summarize protein intensities per sample (`prolfqua::LFQDataAggregator`) <br>- For each PTM-feature fit your usual DE model (`prolfqua::build_model`)<br>- OR: fit DE model to protein and PTM features, then compute log₂FC(PTM) − log₂FC(protein) and recalculate p-values (`prophosqua::test_diff_diff`). |
